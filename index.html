About

I am Shaun Bolak, a freelance Graphic and Web Designer.

When I am not ruling the world, I am creating projects that make it better. I am an artist at heart. So naturally I love design; especially, Logo and Web Design.

Javascript, React, Redux, Sass, Bootstrap, HTML5, JS plugins, 3rd Party APIs, AJAX, FETCH are a few of my skills. Nevertheless, I still approach each new project as I do with any new venture in life - with humility and an attitude that there is always something to learn.

Some examples of my work are posted below. If you would like to work with me, please contact me. I would love to help you design and build your next project!
